Our history

La Perrada de Chalo opened it’s doors for the first time in 1995 at 78th st on Roosevelt Ave, with the idea and nostalgia to bring our Colombian hot dogs, hamburgers, empanadas, and much more to our community in New York, we started in a small place and thanks to our clients acceptability we moved to 83rd St and Northern Blvd.

To this day we have had our doors opened, where at the beginning most of our clients were 100% Latin, and through the years, with our menu variety we count now with clients from all over the world exploring our dishes with a little bit of what is Colombia.
